## Content Summary

**Lesson Topic:** Understanding Financial Markets & Asset Classes

**Learning Objectives:**
1. Learn about different financial markets and their characteristics
2. Understand various asset classes available for trading
3. Recognize trading hours and market behaviors across different markets
4. Identify which markets align with your trading goals and risk tolerance

**Content Sections:**
1. **Stock Markets** - Equity trading fundamentals, trading hours (9:30-4:00 PM ET)
2. **Forex Markets** - Currency trading, 24/5 operations, $6 trillion daily volume
3. **Commodities** - Physical assets (gold, oil, wheat), futures and ETFs
4. **Cryptocurrency** - Digital assets, 24/7 trading, high volatility

**Key Takeaways:**
1. Different markets offer varying levels of risk, volatility, and opportunity
2. Stock markets provide ownership stakes with specific trading hours
3. Forex markets offer 24-hour trading with high liquidity
4. Commodity markets allow trading in physical goods through various instruments
5. Understanding market characteristics helps you choose appropriate trading venues
