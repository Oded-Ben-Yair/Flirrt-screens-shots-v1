## üéØ Content Focus Areas

### Risk Management Foundation
- Why capital preservation matters more than profits
- The 1% rule for professional traders
- How losing streaks are inevitable and manageable

### Position Sizing
- Fixed percentage method (risk 1-3% per trade)
- Calculation formula: `Position Size = Risk Amount / Distance (Entry - Stop)`
- Volatility-based sizing for dynamic adjustments
- Real example: $10k account, 2% risk, 50-pip stop = 2 lots

### Stop Loss Strategies
- Technical stops (support/resistance levels)
- Percentage-based stops (fixed distance from entry)
- ATR-based stops (accounting for volatility)
- Trailing stops (locking in profits as trades move favorably)

### Risk-Reward Ratios
- 2:1 ratio: Risk $1 to make $2
- 3:1 ratio: Risk $1 to make $3
- Profitability possible with <50% win rate when RR ratio is favorable
- Multiple take profit levels for gradual profit taking
