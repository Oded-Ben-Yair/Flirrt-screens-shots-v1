## Content Focus: Trading Psychology & Mindset

### Learning Objectives (4):
1. Understand the psychological challenges of trading
2. Learn to manage emotions like fear and greed
3. Develop discipline and patience in trading decisions
4. Create mental frameworks for consistent decision-making under pressure

### Key Topics Covered:
- **Fear & Greed**: Primary emotions driving market behavior
- **FOMO**: Fear of missing out - chasing prices at poor levels
- **Discipline**: Following predetermined plans regardless of emotions
- **Patience**: Waiting for high-probability setups
- **Routine & Consistency**: Building discipline through repetition
- **Trade Journaling**: Documenting trades for accountability & learning
- **Mental Resilience**: Handling stress, uncertainty, and setbacks
- **Stress Management**: Breathing exercises, meditation, physical activity
- **Accepting Uncertainty**: Focus on risk management, not outcome prediction
- **Learning from Mistakes**: Building resilience through failure analysis

### Exercise:
**Emotion Journal Tracking** - 1 week emotional state monitoring:
- Before trading: Record emotional state
- During trading: Note emotional changes
- After trading: Reflect on outcomes
- Pattern Recognition: Identify emotional patterns
- Awareness Building: Foundation for emotional control
