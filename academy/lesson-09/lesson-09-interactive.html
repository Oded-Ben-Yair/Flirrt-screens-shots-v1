Day Trading Strategies and Techniques

Day trading involves opening and closing positions within the same trading day, avoiding overnight risk but requiring active monitoring and quick decisions. Scalping represents the shortest-term approach with trades lasting minutes or seconds, profiting from small movements with large positions in highly liquid markets. Momentum trading capitalizes on strong price movements triggered by news, earnings, or technical breakouts. Range trading works in sideways markets where prices oscillate between support and resistance. Gap trading focuses on price gaps between sessions, profiting from gap fills or extensions.
