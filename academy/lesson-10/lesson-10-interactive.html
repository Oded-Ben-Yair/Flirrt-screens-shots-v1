Options Trading Strategies and Applications

Options provide powerful tools for income generation, hedging positions, and creating asymmetric risk-reward profiles. Covered call writing involves selling call options against stock positions, generating income with limited downside protection - works well in neutral to slightly bullish markets but limits upside. Cash-secured puts generate income while potentially acquiring stocks at desired prices. Protective puts act as insurance for stock positions, limiting downside risk while maintaining unlimited upside. Spread strategies like bull call spreads and bear put spreads profit from moderate directional moves with defined risk parameters. Iron condors and butterflies profit from low volatility by selling options at different strikes.

Futures contracts are inherently leveraged where small price movements create large profit or loss swings. Leverage management involves calculating position sizes based on actual risk rather than margin required.
