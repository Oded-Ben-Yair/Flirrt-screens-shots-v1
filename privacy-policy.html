Privacy Policy for Flirrt.AI

Last Updated: October 11, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Flirrt.AI ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our iOS application. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use the Flirrt.AI keyboard extension and main app.

Important: Flirrt.AI is designed for users 18 years and older. By using our app, you confirm that you are at least 18 years of age.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Screenshot Data

  • What We Collect: When you take a screenshot of a dating profile or conversation, our app temporarily analyzes the image to generate personalized conversation suggestions.
  • How We Use It: Screenshots are processed by AI to extract context (profile details, conversation content) and generate relevant suggestions.
  • Data Retention: Screenshots are analyzed in real-time and NOT stored permanently. Image data is deleted immediately after analysis (within seconds).

2.2 Voice Data

  • What We Collect: If you choose to use voice features, we collect voice recordings you provide for voice cloning.
  • How We Use It: Voice recordings are sent to ElevenLabs (our voice synthesis partner) to create your personalized voice clone.
  • Consent Required: Voice cloning requires explicit consent. You can withdraw consent and delete your voice data at any time.
  • Data Retention: Voice samples are retained only while the voice cloning feature is active. You can delete them permanently from settings.

2.3 User Preferences and Personalization

  • What We Collect: Your flirt style preferences, selected suggestions, and app usage patterns.
  • How We Use It: To personalize suggestion quality and match your communication style.
  • Data Retention: Stored locally on your device using App Groups. Never shared with third parties.

2.4 Authentication Data

  • What We Collect: Email address, authentication tokens (if you sign in).
  • How We Use It: To provide account access across devices and sync your preferences.
  • Data Retention: Stored securely with industry-standard encryption until you delete your account.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • AI-Powered Suggestions: Analyze screenshots to generate personalized conversation openers and replies
  • Voice Synthesis: Create voice messages using your cloned voice (with consent)
  • Personalization: Learn your preferences to improve suggestion quality over time
  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance app features and performance
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraudulent or abusive use of our services

4. Third-Party Services

Flirrt.AI uses the following third-party services to provide functionality:

4.1 Grok API (xAI)

  • Purpose: AI-powered screenshot analysis and text generation
  • Data Shared: Screenshot images (temporarily), conversation context
  • Privacy Policy: https://x.ai/legal/privacy-policy

4.2 ElevenLabs

  • Purpose: Voice cloning and voice message synthesis
  • Data Shared: Voice recordings (with your consent only)
  • Privacy Policy: https://elevenlabs.io/privacy

4.3 Google Gemini API

  • Purpose: Advanced image analysis and content understanding
  • Data Shared: Screenshot images (temporarily)
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • No Permanent Storage: Screenshots are analyzed in real-time and immediately deleted
  • Secure APIs: All third-party API communications are encrypted and authenticated
  • Local Storage: Sensitive preferences stored locally on your device using iOS Keychain and App Groups
  • Minimal Data Retention: We only keep data necessary for app functionality

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View what data we have collected about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of all your data at any time
  • Opt-Out: Disable voice cloning, screenshot analysis, or other features
  • Data Portability: Export your preferences and usage data
  • Consent Withdrawal: Revoke consent for voice cloning or data processing

To exercise these rights: Contact us at privacy@flirrt.ai or use the in-app settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Flirrt.AI is NOT intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we discover that a user under 18 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

8. Data Retention

  • Screenshots: Analyzed and deleted within seconds (never stored permanently)
  • Voice Recordings: Retained until you delete your voice clone or account
  • User Preferences: Stored locally until you delete the app or reset preferences
  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized data may be retained for service improvement

9. International Users

Flirrt.AI is operated in the United States. If you are accessing the app from outside the US, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the US where our servers are located. By using the app, you consent to this transfer.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new privacy policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@flirrt.ai
  • Support: support@flirrt.ai
